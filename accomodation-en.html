Explore our apartments in our private facility

Discover the charm of our villa apartments located 400 meters from the sea (a 10-minute walk from the beautiful Ocean Breeze, Fortamu Beach, and the famous Island of Love). We offer 3 apartments by the pool. The ground-floor apartment has a fully equipped kitchen with a refrigerator and oven, a terrace, and two triple rooms, each with independent entrances and its own private bathroom. The other two apartments each have a triple room with a private bathroom and a veranda with a fully equipped kitchenette. Our Nyumba Watamu, with its comfort and privacy, will meet your expectations.Our accommodations provide 24-hour surveillance for your peace of mind, with security cameras and a night guard, ensuring a safe and secure environment at any time of day or night.

Relax in our pool, perfect for a peaceful swim or an afternoon in the sun. Included in your stay are Wi-Fi, daily housekeeping service, bi-weekly linen changes, and pool towels. Each bathroom is equipped with a hairdryer and power adapter. Each apartment, as mentioned, has a fully equipped kitchenette, allowing you to manage your stay with total autonomy and freedom. However, if you wish, we offer the option to enjoy daily local dishes featuring fish, meat, and fresh fruit prepared by our skilled chefs, with half-board service available.

Last but not least, you can take advantage of our on-site presence 24/7 to meet your every need. Upon request, we can also arrange transfers to and from Mombasa and Malindi airports with certified drivers who will take you directly to our property. We also organize all the excursions you desire upon request. All you have to do is relax and enjoy your vacation!
