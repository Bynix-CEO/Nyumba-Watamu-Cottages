Our Story

At 7 years old, 1981

At 50 years old, now

In 1981, when I was only 7 years old, my parents took me to Kenya. As soon as I landed in Mombasa and stepped off the plane, I immediately felt a strange sensation wash over me. I fell in love with this country instantly. The beautiful sea with its thousand shades, the landscape, the mud houses along the road that I had only seen in documentaries, the vegetation, the spectacular wildlife both on land and in the sea, the humble locals always smiling, those children with big eyes looking at me and wanting to play with me on the beach, that fragrant and flavorful fruit that I had never seen or tasted in Italy… You have surely heard of “Mal d’Africa,” and yes, once it gets into your soul, it never leaves.

After finishing my studies, I applied to be a flight attendant for Eurofly, which at the time was an Italian company that operated charter flights, and I was fortunate enough to be hired on a fixed-term contract based in Milan. Thus, my dream of traveling became a reality. I visited beautiful places like Mexico, the Dominican Republic, Cuba, Tanzania, Kenya, Guadeloupe, Thailand, and the Maldives…

After that experience, I worked for another 15 years as a flight attendant for Alitalia. But my dream was to live in Kenya, so my husband and I took the big step and moved to Watamu, where we realized our dream called Nyumba Watamu to host you and share this wonderful country with you.
